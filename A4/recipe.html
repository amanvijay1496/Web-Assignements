Pani Puri Recipe

Popular strret food of India

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 medium potatoes
  • 1 small medium onion
  • 1 to 1.5 tablespoon coriander leaves
  • 1 tablespoon chaat masala powder
  • 1/4 tablespoon red chili powder
  • Black salt or regular salt

Steps to make pani puri

  1. boil the potatoes till they are cooked completely
  2. peel them and then chop them
  3. finely chop the onion if using it
  4. mix the potatoes, onions, coriander leaves, chaat masala
